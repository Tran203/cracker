About Us

Mission Statement

Our mission is to transform healthcare delivery by providing accessible, efficient, and patient-centric services through our Q-minimiser system. We are committed to improving the health and well-being of individuals in Tshwane Municipality and beyond, ensuring that every patient receives the care they deserve, when and where they need it. We strive to empower patients, support healthcare providers, and enhance the overall healthcare experience, one appointment, one queue, and one click at a time.

Vision Statement

Our vision is a healthcare landscape where convenience, compassion, and quality intersect seamlessly. We aspire to be the leading innovators in healthcare technology, recognized for our unwavering dedication to patient satisfaction and healthcare excellence. We envision a future where waiting times are minimized, access to medical records is effortless, and healthcare services are as diverse as the communities we serve. Together, we aim to revolutionize healthcare, forging a healthier, happier, and more connected world.

Values

Patient-Centric Care: We prioritize the needs, preferences, and well-being of patients in all that we do, ensuring that their healthcare experience is at the center of our focus. Innovation: We embrace innovation as a means to continuously improve our services, leveraging technology and creative solutions to enhance healthcare accessibility and efficiency. Empowerment: We empower patients by providing them with the tools and information they need to make informed decisions about their healthcare, fostering a sense of control and autonomy.
